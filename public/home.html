Resume Analyzer

Evolve with your resume

This project proposes the development of a web-based intelligent resume builder with ATS scoring and career recommendation features. The system allows students to create accounts and securely store their details in a database. Using the input information such as personal data, education, skills, and projects, the application automatically generates a structured resume in PDF format. An ATS scoring engine evaluates the resume based on keywords, structure, and completeness, and provides a score out of 100 along with feedback. Beyond resume generation, the system includes a career mentoring module, which suggests suitable projects, internships, and online courses from platforms like NPTEL, Coursera, and Udemy based on the student’s background and skills. This helps students bridge gaps, improve their resumes, and become more industry-ready. Users can update their information at any time, after which the system regenerates the resume and recalculates the ATS score, ensuring continuous improvement . Overall, the proposed platform not only simplifies resume creation but also empowers students with personalized career guidance, making it a complete tool for skill development and employability enhancement.
