Resume Analyzer

Evolve with your resume

This project proposes the development of a web-based intelligent resume builder with ATS scoring and career recommendation features. The system allows students to create accounts and securely store their details in a database. Using the input information such as personal data, education, skills, and projects, the application automatically generates a structured resume in PDF format. An ATS scoring engine evaluates the resume based on keywords, structure, and completeness, and provides a score out of 100 along with feedback.
